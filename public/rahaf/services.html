Our Service

We are committed to providing the highest quality patient care in a friendly, compassionate setting.

Teeth Whitening

lightens teeth and helps to remove stains and discoloration.

Teeth Cleaning

part of oral hygiene and involves the removal of dental plaque from teeth.

Quality Brackets

Traditional braces are made with high-quality stainless steel and straighten teeth using metal brackets, wires, and rubber bands.

Modern Anesthetic

used in most dental procedures to keep you from feeling discomfort while we fix your teeth.

Dental Calculus

is a form of hardened dental plaque. It is caused by precipitation of minerals from saliva and gingival crevicular fluid (GCF) in plaque on the teeth.

Paradontosis

any disease of periodontal tissues.

Dental Implants

surgical component that interfaces with the bone of the jaw or skull to support a dental prosthesis.

Tooth Braces

are devices used in orthodontics that align and straighten teeth and help position them with regard to a person's bite, while also aiming to improve dental health.
